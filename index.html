Manage Your Horse's Asthma with Stableize


Get Started

Did you know that 1 in 5 horses have asthma, yet horse owners everywhere struggle to manage their horse’s asthma?

That’s why we’ve created Stableize: an all-inclusive platform created to assist horse caretakers in monitoring asthma symptoms, tracking medications, and making asthma management easier and more effective overall. Stabilize features symptom tracking, weather monitoring, medication reminders and more all in one, easy-to-access location.

F.A.Q

  • Can I set medication reminders on this app?

    Yes! This app contains a medication reminder feature under the 'reminders' tab.

  • Do I have to make an account to use the app?

    Yes. You, as a horse owner or veterinarian, will make one account where you are able to hold information about multiple horses.

  • Am I able to share this information with my vet?

    Yes! There is an option available to share the data you have collected with the person of your choosing.

  • Can I track asthma information for more than one horse on my account?

    Yes! Under one, main account, you are able to track data for as many horses as you would like.

  • Can I view weather predictions on the app?

    Yes! Underneath the weather predictions tab, you will be able to view temperature, pollen counts and more!
